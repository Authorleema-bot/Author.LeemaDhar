About Dr. Leema Dhar

Welcome to My World of Words

Dr. Leema Dhar is an acclaimed Indian author, academic, and educator currently serving as Assistant Professor at K.C. College of Engineering & Management Studies and Research in Mumbai. With over 3 years of teaching experience, including roles at Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology and Charuchandra College, University of Calcutta, she specializes in feminist and postcolonial literature.

With a PhD in English Literature from Central University of Allahabad, Dr. Dhar has published numerous research papers in high-impact journals and authored seven books—two poetry anthologies and five fiction works, including four national bestsellers.

UGC-compliant with an API score exceeding 619, she blends rigorous scholarship with creative expression, mentoring students, advocating for gender equity, and inspiring through motivational speaking to foster critical thinkers and empowered voices in academia and beyond.

Teaching Philosophy

Dr. Dhar's teaching methodology focuses on creating an interactive classroom environment where students are active participants in their learning journey. This approach includes group discussions, real-world problem solving, and collaborative projects that enhance critical thinking skills.

Through innovative techniques, she helps students develop a deep appreciation for literature. Her methods include contextual analysis, comparative studies, and creative writing exercises that make literature accessible and engaging for all learners.
